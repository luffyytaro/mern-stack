Delicious Chocolate Chip Cookies

Preperation time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter,softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degree F(175 degree C).
  2. In a large bowl,cream together the butter and sugar untill smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time,then stir in the vanilla.
  4. In a seperate bowl,combine the flour and baking soda; gradually add to the butter mixture.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by rounded tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or untill edges are golden brown.
